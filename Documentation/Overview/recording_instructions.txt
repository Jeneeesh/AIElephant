
1. Record the MALAYALAM WORDS in separate files. (English words are given for reference.)

2. Name EACH WORD as "audio<num>.ext" where <num> is the serial number given in the pdf list

3. You can record using a sound recording app in your mobile phone. 

4. Record at sampling frequency: 44.1kHz 

5. Recording format: m4a, mp3, or wav (preferred: .wav)
